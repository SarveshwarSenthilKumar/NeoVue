The Vision

  • Redefining eyewear with built-in transparent screens visible only to the user
  • Smart glasses that integrate augmented reality, AI, and real-time utility
  • Displays notifications, directions, time, translations, and more in the user's vision
  • Features voice command, emergency collision detection, and live camera/microphone access
  • Designed for everyday wear—compatible with prescription lenses or non-corrective glasses
"Augmenting Vision, Enhancing Reality."

Why This Matters

  • Eliminates need to constantly check phones—hands-free access to digital life
  • Useful in emergencies—can automatically call for help upon sensing collisions
  • Enhances lives of users with disabilities through navigation and communication support
  • Future potential to integrate with neural interfaces like Neuralink for immersive connectivity
